Nth — Support

Nth counts the reels and shorts you watch and helps you stay under a daily budget. Here's how to get help.

Contact

Questions, bugs, or feedback? Email feenix2006@icloud.com and we'll get back to you.

Common questions

How does Nth count reels?

You browse Instagram and YouTube inside Nth, under your own account. As you scroll, Nth counts each short-form video against your daily budget. The number counts down; at zero you get a gentle pause, never a lockout.

How do I change my daily budget?

Tap the gear icon to open Settings, then pick a preset or use the +/- stepper.

What happens when I hit zero?

Nth asks you to take one slow breath, then the choice is yours — keep going, spin the wheel for a few capped bonus reels, or call it a day and grow your streak.

Is my data private?

Yes. Nth collects nothing — no accounts, no tracking. Everything stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.

Why do I need to log in to Instagram?

Instagram requires an account to view Reels. You log in to Instagram directly inside the app, exactly as you would in Safari — Nth never sees your credentials. YouTube Shorts work without logging in.
