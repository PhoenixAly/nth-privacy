Nth — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 14, 2026

The short version: Nth collects no data. There are no accounts, and nothing you do in the app leaves your device.

What we collect

Nothing. Nth has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no crash reporting. We never see your browsing, your identity, or your social media logins.

What stays on your device

Your daily budget, watch counts, streak history, and the identifiers of videos already counted today are stored locally on your device only, and can be erased at any time by deleting the app.

Third-party websites

Nth is a browser: it displays the mobile websites of services such as Instagram and YouTube. When you browse those sites, you interact with them directly under your own account and their own terms of service and privacy policies — exactly as you would in Safari. Nth does not intercept, store, or transmit your credentials or your activity on those sites; login cookies are held by the operating system's web view on your device.

Nth is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Instagram, Meta, YouTube, or Google.

Changes

If a future version ever collects or shares data (for example, an optional social feature), this policy will be updated first and the change will be called out clearly in the app before you opt in.
